What is a Sauna?

A sauna is a heated room designed to make you sweat and promote recovery.

Benefits

  1. Physical benefits
    • Improves circulation
    • Muscle recovery
    • Cardiovascular health
  2. Mental benefits
    • Stress relief
    • Better sleep
    • Relaxation

Types of Saunas

  • Traditional Finnish Sauna
  • Infrared Sauna
  • Steam Room
